# Bill of Materials

| Item | Quantity | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| ESP32 development board | 1 | Main controller |
| BioAmp EXG Pill | 1 | EMG sensor input |
| MG90S servo motor | 5 | Finger actuation |
| External 5V power supply | 1 | Servo power source |
| Breadboard or terminal block | 1 | Prototyping or wiring |
| Jumper wires | Several | Signal and power wiring |
| EMG electrodes | 1 set | For forearm placement |
| Prosthetic hand frame | 1 | Mechanical hand body |
| Fasteners / brackets | As needed | Servo and linkage mounting |

## Recommended Extras

- Inline power switch
- Fuse on the 5V servo rail
- Capacitor across the servo supply rail
- Cable ties or strain relief

## Notes

- MG90S servos are compact and suitable for finger joints, but they still need a stable 5V rail.
- The exact power supply current depends on the load and how many servos move at once.
